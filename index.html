    Chairman's Message

    We look forward to welcoming you at the 17th Asia Pacific Vascular Intervention Course at New Delhi, India in June 2025! All the members and Non-members of the Asia Pacific Vascular Society can attend the meeting and utilize this scientific platform to excel in their intervention skills. This meeting covers a broad range of expertise. From Interventional Cardiologists to Vascular surgeons, Interventional Radiologists, Interventional Nephrologists, Plastic Surgeons, Endocrinologists, Vascular Physicians and such like, can attend this meeting and step-up their knowledge and experience. We would like to encourage you to join the meeting as a member of the Society to stay involved in every activity. Hope to see you all at the APVIC XVII
